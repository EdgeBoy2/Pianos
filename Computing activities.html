"The piano is such a great instrument. So versatile, and it has such a large range" - Daniel Cash
As said by the great Daniel Cash himself, the piano is very good.

The notes on the piano are:

  1. A
  2. A# or Bb
  3. B
  4. C
  5. C# or Db
  6. D
  7. D# or Eb
  8. E
  9. F
  10. F# or Gb
  11. G
  12. G# or Ab

Then back round to A!

Pianos are known for being:

  • Versatile (as quoted by Daniel Cash)
  • Easy to learn
  • And being the best instrument in the world!
